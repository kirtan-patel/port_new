about

I've always been a story-teller first, long before i stumbled upon photgraphy.It all started with writing stilly imaginary pieces when i was younger, until the day I picked up a camera and discovered medium to express myself.

Capturing emotions is one of the most important part of my work. I feel strongly about making the photographs that I can connect with. Even though it is the emotions of my subjects that I'm documenting, I find a reflection of myself in them.

There's no suprise that photography is the love of my life, but traveling is also the blood in my veins. I'm endlessly inspired by nature and adventures, I love waking up to new horizons, making new friends, and exploring the world.
